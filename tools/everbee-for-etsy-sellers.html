Etsy product and keyword research

Ad review draft: EverBee for Etsy sellers

EverBee Research can help compare product, shop and keyword signals using estimated marketplace data. Those figures are research inputs—not audited competitor accounts, guaranteed search positions or promised sales.

Quick verdict

Possible fit: an Etsy seller comparing product demand, competing listings or keyword opportunities before creating or revising a listing.

Skip or delay it: if your immediate problem is bookkeeping, fulfilment, photography or an offer buyers do not want—or if you expect a research tool to guarantee a profitable product.

QuarterReady's recommendation

Use the free Hobby plan to investigate one genuine product decision. Upgrade only if the additional estimated data would change decisions you are ready to act on repeatedly.

What EverBee Research currently provides

EverBee's current Hobby plan is free and requires no payment card. Its published plan information includes limited product analytics, keyword research and filters. The paid Growth plan adds estimated sales, revenue and conversion-rate data, unlimited keyword research with a keyword score, a tag analyser and larger research limits.

EverBee also sells separate email and storefront products. Do not assume an EverBee Research subscription includes every feature in the wider EverBee product family.

A free-plan product test

  1. Choose one product idea or listing decision—not an entire shop redesign.
  2. Read the provider's permissions and privacy terms before installing the extension or connecting an Etsy account.
  3. Compare several relevant listings and keywords, looking at demand, competition, reviews, favourites and visible trends rather than one headline number.
  4. Write down the decision the research changes: proceed, narrow the niche, change the offer or stop.
  5. Pay only when the free limits repeatedly hide estimated data needed for real decisions.

Important limitations

  • Treat third-party sales, revenue and conversion figures as estimates, not audited records from another Etsy shop.
  • Product and keyword data cannot guarantee Etsy ranking, demand, conversion or profit.
  • Strong research cannot fix weak product quality, photography, pricing, delivery or customer service.
  • The workflow uses a browser extension and an Etsy connection; review EverBee's requested access and privacy policy first.
  • Paid subscriptions renew under the provider's current terms unless cancelled. Check the current billing period and total charge before buying.
  • QuarterReady has not connected a shop or purchased a plan, so this is a fit guide rather than a hands-on review.
