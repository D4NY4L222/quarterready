Taxfix: a human-filed route when you do not want to file alone

Taxfix, formerly TaxScouts in the UK, offers tax-return preparation and filing through UK-accredited accountants. It also sells separate Making Tax Digital plans. Choose the service for the job you actually need.

Quick verdict

Possible fit: a UK sole trader who wants an accountant to prepare and file a Self Assessment return and values an online, fixed-service workflow.

Skip or compare alternatives: if your return is straightforward and you are comfortable filing directly with HMRC, if you already have an accountant, or if you only need bookkeeping software.

What Taxfix currently offers

The provider says its UK tax-return services are completed by UK-accredited accountants and cover self-employed people alongside other common Self Assessment groups. Taxfix also publishes separate MTD Self-service, Assistance and Full Support plans. Those products differ in price and the amount of accountant involvement.

Affiliate qualification is narrower than the product catalogue

Taxfix's public affiliate page explicitly lists tax returns, tax advice, tax bundles and company tax returns as commission-eligible purchases. It does not explicitly confirm MTD subscriptions. QuarterReady will not claim an MTD referral commission unless Taxfix confirms it in writing.

When human help is worth comparing

  • You have several income sources or records that need explanation.
  • You want somebody qualified to prepare and file the return.
  • You value an online hand-off more than maintaining a long-term local adviser relationship.

A service is not automatically better than HMRC's direct filing route or an established accountant who already knows your business. Compare scope, exclusions, response times and whether advice is included rather than assuming the headline filing price covers every issue.

Before you pay

  1. Confirm the exact service and tax year.
  2. Read what documents and bookkeeping condition the provider expects.
  3. Check which extra or specialist work may be quoted separately.
  4. Verify deadlines and turnaround before leaving a filing close to the deadline.
  5. Keep copies of the submitted return, calculations and supporting records.

Important limitations

  • QuarterReady has not purchased a Taxfix service and is not rating its accountants.
  • Provider pricing, discounts and scope can change.
  • No tax service can guarantee a refund or a particular tax result.
  • Taxfix's MTD product is separate from a one-off Self Assessment service; confirm which one you are buying.
